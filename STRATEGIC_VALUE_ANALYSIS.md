## The Problem We Solve

### Municipal Bond Market Inefficiencies

**Current Market Size:** $4 trillion (U.S. only)

**Critical Problems:**
- **Extreme Illiquidity:** Bonds trade infrequently, hard to buy/sell
- **High Barriers to Entry:** $5,000-$25,000 minimum investments exclude retail investors
- **Slow Settlement:** T+2 to T+3 days (vs. instant on blockchain)
- **Opaque Pricing:** Fragmented markets, unclear spreads
- **Limited Trading Hours:** Market hours only, no 24/7 access
- **Quantum Vulnerability:** All existing platforms use RSA/ECDSA (will be broken by quantum computers)

### The Quantum Threat

**Why This Matters for Bonds:**
- Municipal bonds have **10-30 year maturities**
- Bond issued in 2025 matures in 2045-2055
- **Quantum computers will exist by then**
- Current cryptography (RSA, ECDSA, Bitcoin, Ethereum) will be **completely broken**
- **30-year securities CANNOT use quantum-vulnerable crypto**

**Regulatory Mandate:**
- NIST published post-quantum standards (2024)
- Federal agencies must migrate by 2030-2035
- Financial institutions will be **required** to adopt quantum-safe systems
- **We're the only blockchain platform ready for this transition**
